PROBLEM: Port 5173 already in use
SOLUTION:
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ npx kill-port 5173                             │

└────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

PROBLEM: Prettier not formatting
SOLUTION:
  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+P (Cmd+Shift+P on Mac)
  2. Type: "Preferences: Open Settings"
  3. Search: "format on save"
  4. Check the box

PROBLEM: ESLint not working
SOLUTION:
  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+P (Cmd+Shift+P on Mac)
  2. Type: "ESLint: Restart ESLint Server"
  3. Press Enter

PROBLEM: TypeScript errors everywhere
SOLUTION:
  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+P (Cmd+Shift+P on Mac)
  2. Type: "TypeScript: Restart TS Server"
  3. Press Enter
  4. Wait 10 seconds

PROBLEM: Changes not showing in browser
SOLUTION:
  Hard refresh browser: Ctrl+Shift+R (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
